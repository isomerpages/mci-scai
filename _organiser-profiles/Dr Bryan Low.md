**Dr Bryan LOW**<br>*Associate Professor of Computer Science, National University of Singapore*<br>

---

Bryan Low is an Associate Professor of Computer Science at the National University of Singapore and the Director of AI Research at AI Singapore. He obtained the PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University in 2009. His research interests include data-centric AI, resource-efficient AI, probabilistic &amp; automated machine learning, planning under uncertainty, multi-agent/robot systems, and LLMs. Dr. Low is the recipient of the Andrew P. Sage Best Transactions Paper Award for the best paper published in all 3 of the IEEE Transactions on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics - Parts A, B, and C in 2006.
